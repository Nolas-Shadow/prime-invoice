# Prime Invoice 🏦 ⛓️

### The Infrastructure for Institutional Trade Finance

Prime Invoice is a Decentralized Invoice Marketplace built to bridge the $2.5T+ global supply chain finance gap. By tokenizing corporate invoices into Real-World Assets (RWAs), we provide SMEs with immediate working capital while offering Web3 investors institutional-grade, short-duration yield.

---

## 🚀 The Vision

Traditional Factoring (selling invoices for immediate cash) is broken:
- **Capital Starvation:** SMEs wait 60–90 days for payment, crippling growth.
- **Legacy Gatekeepers:** High fees and manual overhead make mid-market corporate invoices (>$50k) surprisingly underserved by traditional lenders.
- **Fraud Risk:** "Double-financing" errors are common in siloed bank systems.

**Prime Invoice solves this with Autonomous Infrastructure:**
- **Instant Settlement:** Atomic invoice funding in seconds on high-performance blockchain networks.
- **Safety First:** Cryptographic signatures from verified buyers eliminate double-financing fraud.
- **Verified Invoices:** Exclusively focusing on corporate debt (>$50k) from audited, credit-worthy entities.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **Smart Contracts:** Security-first implementation for the **Base (L2)** ecosystem.
- **Business Logic:** Powered by **DAML** for multi-party agreement workflows and legal perfection.
- **Frontend:** Institutional React/Vite-based UI for Suppliers and Financiers.
- **Docs:** Professional **VitePress** documentation portal.
