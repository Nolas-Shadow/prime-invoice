# Introduction

Prime Invoice is a decentralized invoice factoring marketplace built on the **Base (L2)** network. It bridges Real-World Assets (RWA) by tokenizing corporate invoices for immediate liquidity.

## What is Invoice Factoring?

Factoring is the process of selling an invoice to a third party (a financier) at a discount for immediate cash. For SMEs, this is a vital tool for managing cash flow when corporate buyers have long payment cycles (30, 60, or 90 days).

## The Prime Invoice Vision

Prime Invoice leverages Web3 to remove legacy TradFi gatekeepers:
- **Permissionless Yield**: Anyone with USDC can become a Financier.
- **Atomic Settlement**: Funds are moved instantly upon agreement, not in 3-5 business days.
- **Transparency**: Every invoice lifecycle from proposal to repayment is tracked on the Base blockchain.

## Key Participants

### SME Supplier
The business that issued the invoice and needs funding to maintain operations.

### Corporate Buyer
The Fortune 1000 company that owes the debt on the invoice. Their creditworthiness is the core of the asset's value.

### Financier
The liquidity provider who funds the invoice at a discount, earning a yield when the Buyer repays in full.
