The Problem

  • Cash Flow Starvation: SMEs are crippled because large corporate buyers dictate 60-90 day payment cycles.
  • Legacy Gatekeepers: TradFi banks charge massive origination fees, require mountains of paperwork, and outright refuse to service smaller "micro-invoices."
  • The Result: A multi-trillion dollar supply chain market is historically underserved, leaving suppliers desperate for immediate working capital.

The Solution

Prime Invoice is a Decentralized Invoice Marketplace.

  • Real-World Assets (RWAs): We tokenize actual corporate invoices into high-yield, short-duration (30/60/90 day) digital assets.
  • Permissionless Liquidity: Any Web3 user with USDC can step in as a "Financier", earning institutional-grade yield.
  • Cryptographic Truth: Buyers digitally sign the invoice on-chain, mathematically eliminating the risk of "double-financing fraud".
